    Projects

    Academic Projects

    • HFT Master Thesis:Geospatial Approaches for Increasing Water Availability

      Abstract: The sustainable management of water is a crucial issue globally due to its scarcity. While Germany has established efficient water management systems and is regarded to have high water sufficiency among European nations.But the agricultural sector still struggles with water scarcity as the demand surpasses the available water supply. The primary aim is to establish a framework for making water accessible for irrigation and additional use on households through the effective utilization of recycled water from wastewater treatment facilities.

    • Comparison of Simulation Tools for Solar Energy Potential Analysis

      Abstract: Simulation modeling has been playing a significant role in predicting energy transition scenarios. With the rising demnad for real-time solutions, simulation tools are making their way through different stages of energy planning, i.e., from design to the commencement phase. So, the main objective of this research is to compare the workflow and outputs of two simulation tools-SimStadt and SAGA GIS for interpreting incoming solar potentials.

    • Air Mobility

      Abstract: The Urban Air Mobility is defined as the Safe and Efficient Air Traffic Operations in a metropolitan area carried out by aircraft Systems.The main insight of this project is to define an urban taxi route for smart mobility by 3D routing visualization on Cesium ion environment, routing application development, and proposal of futuristic stations for air taxis and demo customer portals.
